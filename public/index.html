Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Orders are processed within 1‑2 business days and shipped via standard USPS. Delivery typically takes 3‑5 days.

Can I order a custom size?

We currently offer three standard sizes. If you need a different size, please contact us and we’ll see what we can do.

How do I upload my photo?

You’ll upload your image when you add a product to the cart. We’ll use that file to print your magnet.
