Shipping & Delivery

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (Free on orders over $299)
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days ($15)
  • Next Day Delivery: 1 business day ($35)
  • Freight Shipping: For large/heavy items (calculated at checkout)

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history, or using the "Track Order" link at the top of the page.

Currently, we ship to the United States and Canada. International shipping to other countries is available for select products. Please contact our sales team for a custom quote on international orders.

Orders & Payment

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Wire Transfer (for orders over $1,000)
  • Net 30 terms (for approved business accounts)

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, orders enter our fulfillment process and cannot be changed. Please contact customer service immediately if you need to make changes.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused, in original packaging, and include all accessories. Some items (custom orders, hazardous materials) are non-returnable. Return shipping is free for defective items.

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. Credit card refunds may take an additional 5-7 business days to appear on your statement depending on your bank.
