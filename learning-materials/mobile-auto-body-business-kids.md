# 🚗 Fixing Car Scratches - For Kids!

*How to start your own car repair business*

---

## 💰 How Much Money Can You Make?

**$600 to $800** for fixing one car!
- Takes about 2-3 hours
- That's like earning $300 per hour! 🤑

If you fix **one car every day**, you could make **$12,000 in a month**!

---

## 🛠️ What Tools Do You Need?

| Tool | What it does | Like a... |
|------|--------------|-----------|
| Air Compressor | Paints the car | Super-powered spray can |
| Sander | Smooths out scratches | Electric sandpaper |
| Spray Gun | Puts on color | Fancy spray bottle |
| Heat Gun | Dries paint fast | Super hairdryer |
| Buffer | Makes car shiny | Spinning sponge |

**Total cost:** About $2,500 (but you don't need to buy everything at first!)

---

## 🎨 How Do You Fix a Car?

### 5 Easy Steps:

**1️⃣ Look at the damage**
- Is it a scratch? A scuff?
- Don't take big jobs - just small ones!

**2️⃣ Prep the spot**
- Sand it smooth
- Like smoothing playdoh 🧱

**3️⃣ Paint the color**
- Match the car color using a code
- Paint just the spot, not the whole car!

**4️⃣ Add clear coat**
- Makes it shiny like the rest of the car ✨

**5️⃣ Buff it up**
- Polish until it looks perfect
- Make the scratch disappear! 🎆

## 👀 How Do You Get Customers?

### The #1 Way: Google!
- Make a Google Business Profile
- Take LOTS of before/after photos
- Ask happy customers for reviews ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Before/After Photos are KEY!**
- Take a photo BEFORE you fix it
- Take a photo AFTER you fix it
- Show everyone what you can do!

---

## 💵 How Do You Set Your Price?

Think of it like 3 buckets:

| Bucket | What it is | Price |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Small | Little scratches | $$ |
| Medium | Bigger scuffs | $$$ |
| Big | Needs filler work | $$$$ |

**Most jobs:** $600-$800
- Not too cheap (you're worth it!)
- Not too expensive (still cheaper than a body shop!)

---

## 🤝 How Do You Talk to Customers?

Here's what to say:

1. **Ask for photos** - "Send me 2-3 pictures of the scratch"
2. **Tell them what you can do** - "Yes! I can fix that!"
3. **Show your work** - "Look at this similar job I did!"
4. **Say the price** - "That will be $650"
5. **Book the time** - "Can you do Tuesday or Wednesday?"

---

## ❌ Big Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Don't take too big jobs** - Stick to small scratches! 🐟
2. **Don't overcomplicate** - Same steps every time
3. **Don't rush** - Do it right or it looks bad
4. **Don't charge too low** - You're a pro! 💪
5. **Don't skip the system** - Have a plan!

---

**One car a day =**

- $3,000 × 4 weeks = **$12,000/month!** 🚀

You just need:
