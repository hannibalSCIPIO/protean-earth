# Sex & Your Body: What Actually Matters
*For 16 year olds*

---

## 🤔 Why Are We Even Talking About This?

Sex is a big deal. It affects your health, your relationships, and your happiness. The better you understand it now, the better decisions you'll make.

---

## 🍎 Fuel (What You Eat)

- **Eat real food**: fruits, veggies, nuts, lean meats
- **Pistachios & blueberries** = good for blood flow (not magic, but helpful)
- **Stay hydrated**
- **Fiber** keeps your blood vessels healthy

> *Fast food and processed foods? They can mess with your hormones over time.*

---

## 🏃 Movement (Exercise)

- Just **150 minutes of cardio per week** = better erections (no joke, research shows it's as effective as Viagra)
- Exercise improves blood flow to *everything*
- Lifting weights = more testosterone
- Your pelvic floor muscles matter more than you think

---

## 😴 Sleep & Stress

**Sleep:**
- 5 hours of sleep vs 8 hours = **15% less testosterone**
- Your body makes testosterone while you sleep

**Stress:**
- Chronic stress kills your sex drive
- Cortisol (stress hormone) blocks testosterone
- Take breaks. Breathe. Live your life.

---

## 🧠 The Brain Part

- You need to be **relaxed** to get an erection
- **Performance anxiety** = can't get it up (it's real)
- Focus on your partner, not "performing"
- If you're distracted by your phone, social media, or stress... you're not really there

---

## 📱 Tech Stuff

- Too much screen time = harder to be present
- Porn ≠ real sex
- Some guys get used to specific porn stimulation and then real life feels different
- If that happens, it's a thing you can work on

---

## 💬 Talking to Your Partner

- Use your words
- Ask what they like
- Don't assume they know what you like
- If something hurts or doesn't feel good, say so

---

## ⚠️ What's Normal?

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Is my penis normal? | Average is ~5.5 inches. Most girls don't care about size—they care about the person. |
| Can't get it up sometimes? | Totally normal. Happens to everyone. |
| Morning erections? | Healthy sign = good blood flow |
| PE (finishing too fast)? | Common. You can work on it. |

---

## 🩺 When to See a Doctor

- If you can't get an erection *ever* (that might signal something else)
- If something hurts
- If you're worried about anything

---

## 🔑 The Bottom Line

1. **Take care of your body** – sleep, food, exercise
2. **Relax** – stress kills the mood
3. **Communicate** – talk to your partner
4. **It's okay to wait** – only do what you're comfortable with
5. **Respect boundaries** – yours and theirs
