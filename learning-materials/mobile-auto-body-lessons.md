# 20 Lessons: Scaling a Mobile Auto Body Business

---

## 1. Simplicity Beats Fancy
- Don't waste money trying to look impressive
- $8K fancy trailer made no more money than $4K setup
- Customers just want their car fixed

## 2. Professionalism Pays
- Keep trailer clean, tools organized
- Show up clean and professional
- Easiest way to stand out and get repeat work

## 3. Simple Scales
- Master a few core services you can deliver flawlessly
- Don't try to do every repair that comes your way
- Cutting back actually helped growth

## 4. Don't Overspend on Websites
- $10K website = same leads as free landing page
- Customers don't care about design
- They just want their problem solved

## 5. You Don't Need a Shop
- Mobile = lower overhead = stronger profits
- Stay mobile, don't confuse overhead with scalability

## 6. Don't Rely on One Account
- Diversify or one customer can sink you
- Add retail customers as backup

## 7. Trust Your Gut on Customers
- Say no to red flag customers
- Problem customers cost more than they pay

## 8. Underpromise, Overdeliver
- Set lowish expectations, then beat them
- Creates fans for life

## 9. Surprise with Extra Value
- Use leftover paint to touch up chips for free
- Small extras turn customers into promoters

## 10. Responsiveness Always Wins
- Reply quickly even if you can't do the job
- People book because you responded

---

## 11. Stop Lowballing
- Raise prices to reflect value, not cost of supplies
- Low-quality customers disappear, profits go up

## 12. Price for Value, Not Hours
- You're selling outcomes, not time
- Charge for the value you provide

## 13. Work for Wealthy Clients
- Wealthy clients know other wealthy clients
- Great work = referrals to their network

## 14. Cold Ads Burn Cash
- Cold ads are a gamble
- Retarget people already interested → that's where the money is

## 15. Email Brings Customers Back
- Automated follow-ups, reminders, check-ins
- Stay top of mind or get forgotten

## 16. Keep Customers in the Loop
- Send quick text updates
- Communication builds trust

## 17. Reviews Sell for You
- Ask happy customers for reviews
- Reviews = free salespeople

## 18. Google My Business = Free Leads
- If you're not on Google, you're invisible
- Set it up, get found locally

## 19. Save for the Slow Season
- Save extra during busy months
- Preparation = wisdom (Proverbs 6: ant storing food)

## 20. Play the Long Game
- Business is built in seasons (planting vs harvesting)
- Success = showing up consistently day after day
- No one big break, just consistent effort

---

*Key theme: Keep it simple, provide value, play the long game.*
