## RISK MITIGATION FOR YOUR SETUP

### Given Your Constraints:

1. **Start with 50% of capital** ($2,500) - keep $2,500 as safety buffer
2. **Test with 10-20 units** per product before scaling
3. **Avoid seasonal items** until you have 3+ months data
4. **Diversify across 3-5 categories** - don't put all money in one product
5. **Check Amazon restrictions** before buying - some brands are gated

### Amazon FBA Specific Risks:

- **Long-term storage fees** - Ship fast-selling items; remove slow movers
- **IP complaints** - Avoid name-brand electronics; stick to generic/unbranded
- **Category approval** - Some categories require approval (apply early)
- **Returns** - Factor 5-10% return rate into margins
